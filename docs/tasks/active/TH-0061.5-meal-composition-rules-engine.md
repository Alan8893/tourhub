# Business Rules

## Breakfast

Composition:

- main dish;
- snacks/additional food;
- drinks.

Example:

- porridge;
- sandwiches;
- tea and coffee.

---

## Lunch

Composition:

- main dish;
- snacks/additional food;
- drinks.

Example:

- borscht;
- sandwiches;
- compote.

---

## Snack

Priority:

1. fruits;
2. dried fruits;
3. sweets;
4. cookies;
5. energy bars;
6. sandwiches rarely.

---

## Dinner

Composition:

- main dish;
- salad/additional food;
- drinks.

Example:

- merchant-style buckwheat;
- vitamin salad;
- tea and coffee.

---

# Selection Priority

1. Instructor favorite dishes.
2. Season compatibility.
3. Product availability.
4. Menu diversity.
5. Cooking simplicity.
6. Weight and transportation convenience.
